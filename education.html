Education Summary

Embarking on my educational odyssey at Bharat Bharati Public School, Godda, was like stepping into the first chapter of a captivating novel. Guided by an innate intuitiveness, I maneuvered through challenges with a keen discernment that set the stage for my current pursuit of innovation. The five years at Delhi Public School Ranchi from 2013 to 2018 unfolded as an invaluable saga of personal and academic growth. The school's nurturing environment became the crucible for my passion for learning, fostering an unwavering drive for excellence. The pivotal 9th to 12th grade years at Indian Heritage School under the CBSE Board were a symphony of personal and academic crescendos, laying a robust foundation for my ongoing journey of innovation and learning. Now, at VIT, Vellore, my engineering narrative is a transformative masterpiece. Immersed in a dynamic learning atmosphere, I have honed technical expertise, embraced innovation, and nurtured leadership skills, sculpting a trajectory for a future marked by success in the ever-evolving engineering realm. Each educational chapter has been a brushstroke, contributing to the canvas of my unique and fascinating journey.
